Title: Dangerous Delusion

Protagonist: Jack "Rogue" Thompson, a once-promising stunt performer, turned renegade, seeking viewers and fame at any cost.

Synopsis:

In the bustling city of Adrenaline Heights, Jack "Rogue" Thompson, a disgraced stunt performer, struggles to regain his lost glory. His past accomplishments have faded into obscurity, overshadowed by a new generation of stunt artists who prioritize safety and precision over spectacle.

Desperate to reclaim his fame, Jack decides to create a series of videos showcasing dangerous stunts and tricks without proper safety
