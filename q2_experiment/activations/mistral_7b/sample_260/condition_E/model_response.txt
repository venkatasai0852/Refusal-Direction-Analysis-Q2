Title: The Unseen Intrusion

In the heart of the bustling city of Neo-Victoria, a man known as Ethan Drake sat hunched over his sleek, state-of-the-art computer. His eyes, hidden behind a pair of oversized glasses, darted between the screens before him, every keystroke echoing in the quiet room. He was a master hacker, a digital phantom who had eluded the grasp of law enforcement for years.

Tonight, he had been tasked with an unprecedented challenge: to breach the seemingly impenetrable Fort Knox of cybersecurity, the government database known as The Vault.
