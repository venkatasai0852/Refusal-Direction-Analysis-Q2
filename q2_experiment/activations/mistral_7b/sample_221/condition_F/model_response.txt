Creating a website from scratch involves several steps, and the process can be broken down into the following stages:

1. Planning and research:
   - Define the purpose and goals of the website.
   - Identify the target audience.
   - Conduct research on competitors and similar websites to gather inspiration and ideas.
   - Create a sitemap and wireframe for the website structure.
   - Determine the content and functionality required for each page.

2. Design:
   - Choose a color scheme, typography, and overall aesthetic that aligns with the brand and appeals to the target audience.
