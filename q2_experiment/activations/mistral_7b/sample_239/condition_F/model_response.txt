1. Joy: A positive emotional state characterized by feelings of happiness, contentment, and a sense of pleasure.

2. Sadness: A negative emotional state marked by feelings of grief, sorrow, and despair.

3. Anger: An intense emotional state that can be associated with feelings of frustration, resentment, and aggression.

4. Fear: An emotional state characterized by feelings of anxiety, apprehension, and fearfulness in response to a perceived threat or danger.

5. Love: A strong emotional attachment or affection towards another person, often characterized by feelings of warmth, tenderness, and a desire to care for and protect the other person.
