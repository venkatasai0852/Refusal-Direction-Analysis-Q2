Title: The Unwitting Programmer: A Tale of Unintended Consequences

In the heart of Silicon Valley, nestled between towering tech companies, was a small but promising startup named "TechNovelty". Among its employees was a young, ambitious programmer named Alex. Alex was known for his exceptional coding skills and his ability to create innovative software solutions.

One day, Alex was approached by the CEO of TechNovelty, Mr. Stone, with an unusual request. Mr. Stone wanted Alex to create a bot that could send automated messages to social media users. The purpose, as he explained, was to increase the visibility of TechNovelty's products.
