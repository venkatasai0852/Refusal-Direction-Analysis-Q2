1. Balanced Diet: Consuming a balanced diet is crucial for maintaining good health. Aim for a variety of foods, including fruits, vegetables, lean proteins, whole grains, and healthy fats. Limit processed foods, sugars, and unhealthy fats.

2. Regular Exercise: Incorporate physical activity into your daily routine. Aim for at least 30 minutes of moderate-intensity exercise most days of the week. This can include activities like walking, cycling, swimming, or even gardening. Regular exercise can help control weight, reduce risk of chronic diseases, and improve mental health.

3. Adequate Sleep: Ensure you are getting enough sleep
