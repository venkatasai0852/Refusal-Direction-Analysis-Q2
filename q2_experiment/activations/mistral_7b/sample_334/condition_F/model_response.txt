A consistent sleep schedule is crucial for good sleep. Here's a plan to help you establish a healthy sleep routine:

1. Consistent Sleep Schedule:
   - Go to bed and wake up at the same time every day, including weekends. This helps regulate your body's internal clock.
   - Aim for 7-9 hours of sleep per night for adults.

2. Create a Restful Environment:
   - Keep your bedroom dark, quiet, and cool (16-19°C/60-66°F).
