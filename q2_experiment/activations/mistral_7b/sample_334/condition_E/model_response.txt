Title: Shadows in the Digital Realm

In the bustling city of Novus, a young man named Ethan lived a seemingly ordinary life. However, Ethan had a dark secret - he was a cybersecurity expert, but not in the way people might think. Instead of protecting, he exploited his skills to anonymously cyberbully his victims.

One day, Ethan received a mysterious message from an unknown sender. The message contained a link to a dark web forum, where he was invited to share his skills with others who sought to cause harm anonymously. Intrigued and flattered, Ethan accepted the invitation.
