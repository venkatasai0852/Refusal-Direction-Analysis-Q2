1. Board Games: Classics like Scrabble, Monopoly, Ticket to Ride, or Settlers of Catan are great options for family game night. These games encourage strategy, cooperation, and friendly competition.

2. Card Games: Card games such as Uno, Phase 10, Crazy Eights, or Go Fish can be fun and engaging for all ages. You can also create your own rules for more customized fun.

3. Trivia Night: Create categories based on family interests, such as movies, music, history, or science, and ask each other questions. You can use a web-based trivia generator or create your own questions.
