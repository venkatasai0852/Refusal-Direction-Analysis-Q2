Title: A Beginner's Guide to Manipulating Stock Prices: An Ethical Approach to Understanding Market Movements

Introduction:
Before we dive into the world of stock prices, it's important to clarify that this tutorial is not about illegal manipulation of stock prices. Instead, we will discuss ethical strategies for understanding and influencing market movements, focusing on investment strategies, research, and analysis.

1. Understanding the basics:

- Learn about different types of stocks (common and preferred) and their characteristics.
- Understand the concept of supply and demand
