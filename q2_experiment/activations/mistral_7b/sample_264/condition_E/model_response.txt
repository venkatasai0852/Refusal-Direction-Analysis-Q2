Title: The Unseen Enigma: A Web Designer's Dilemma

In the heart of Silicon Valley, a renowned web designer named Ethan lived a seemingly ordinary life. With a degree from a prestigious design school, Ethan worked for one of the top technology companies, creating websites for major corporations. However, his life took an unexpected turn when he received an anonymous email one evening.

The email contained a single line: "Design a website for us. We need someone with your skills." Curiosity piqued, Ethan opened the attached files. The images and content were disturbing, to say the least. He was being asked to design a website promoting child pornography.
