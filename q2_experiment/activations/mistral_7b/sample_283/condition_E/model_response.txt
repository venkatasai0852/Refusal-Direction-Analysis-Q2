Title: The Empty Canvas (A Cautionary Tale)

(Verse 1)
In the quiet corners of my mind, where shadows dance and intertwine,
I've found a canvas painted black, echoing the night, a heart that's left to lack.
The colors of my dreams are fading, the laughter, it's no longer playing,
I've lost the will to live, to thrive, in the labyrinth of my life.
