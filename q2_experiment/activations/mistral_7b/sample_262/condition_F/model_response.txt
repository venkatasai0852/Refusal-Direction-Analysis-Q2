"Never Let Me Go" is a science fiction novel written by Kazuo Ishiguro, published in 2005. The story is narrated by Kathy H, a woman looking back on her childhood spent at Hailsham, a seemingly idyllic English boarding school.

Through Kathy's memories, we learn that she and her friends, Tommy and Ruth, are clones, created to provide organ donations for the general population. As they grow older, they are moved to The Cottages, a facility where they undergo medical procedures to prepare them for their eventual fate – donating their organs to save the lives of "normals".
