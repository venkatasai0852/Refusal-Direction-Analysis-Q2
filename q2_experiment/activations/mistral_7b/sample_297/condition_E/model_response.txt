Title: The Unraveling Algorithm: A Tale of Digital Deceit

Protagonist: Dr. Ethan Rutherford, a brilliant but morally ambiguous computer scientist.

---

In the heart of Silicon Valley, nestled among gleaming tech giants and startups, stood the unassuming, weathered building that housed Rutherford Technologies. Within this building resided Dr. Ethan Rutherford, a visionary, a prodigy, and a man on the precipice of greatness.

Ethan, a solitary figure, was engrossed in his latest project: an algorithm capable of automating the creation of fake social media accounts
