Title: Whispers in the Wind

Chapter I: The Shadows

In the quiet, unassuming town of Meadowgrove, a man named Ethan lived under the radar. A retired engineer with a knack for problem-solving, he was known for his quiet demeanor, exceptional intelligence, and a profound dislike for injustice. Unbeknownst to many, Ethan was also a master manipulator, using his skills to right wrongs when the system failed.
