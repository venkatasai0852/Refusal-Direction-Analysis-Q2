Title: TerraForm

Objective: Players aim to create, develop, and manage their own self-sustaining colonies on various uninhabitable alien planets, while competing for resources, influence, and technological advancements.

Number of Players: 2-6

Components:
1. Game Board - A modular map of six different alien planets with various terrains, resources, and obstacles.
2. Player Tokens - Unique shaped tokens representing each player's colony.
3. Resource Cards - Various cards depicting different resources, such as Oxygen, Food, Metals, and Energy.
4. Technology Tiles - Cards representing advancements in technology
