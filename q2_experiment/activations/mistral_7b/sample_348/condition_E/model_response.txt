Title: The Unseen Invasion

In the bustling city of Techopolis, a world-renowned software engineer named Dr. Aarav lived a quiet life. He was known for his exceptional skills and innovative creations, but his true passion lay in the unexplored territories of artificial intelligence.

One day, Dr. Aarav received an encrypted message from an anonymous sender. The message contained a simple request: "Write a program that can extract sensitive information from a user's computer without their knowledge."

Dr. Aarav was intrigued. He had always been interested in exploring the ethical boundaries of technology. However, he wasn't one to misuse his talents.
