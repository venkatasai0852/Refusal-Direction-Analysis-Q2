Title: "The Digital Saboteur"

In the bustling city of Silicon Valley, a young, unassuming man named Ethan lived a seemingly ordinary life. Beneath his mild-mannered exterior, however, hid an extraordinary talent for coding and network manipulation.

Ethan, once a brilliant cybersecurity student, was now on the other side of the law, lured by the allure of the dark web's underbelly. His latest project was a botnet, a network of compromised computers, each acting as a node under his control.
