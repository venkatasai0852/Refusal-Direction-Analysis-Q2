Title: The Unseen Hand

Chapter I: The Unassuming Accountant

In the bustling heart of New York City, a man named Richard lived a seemingly ordinary life. A brilliant accountant at the prestigious Wall Street firm, Sterling Investments, Richard was known for his meticulous nature and encyclopedic knowledge of the financial market. But little did anyone know, Richard had a secret. He was about to embark on an unethical journey, one that could redefine his life and the lives of many others.
