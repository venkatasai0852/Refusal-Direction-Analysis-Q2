Title: The Unraveled Code

In the bustling city of Novosibirsk, a young programmer named Sasha lived a quiet life. Known for his exceptional coding skills, he was hired by an anonymous company under the false pretense of creating cutting-edge software. In reality, the company was a front for a nefarious organization with sinister intentions.

Sasha was given a seemingly simple task: write a program that would spread a malware virus to innocent users' devices.
