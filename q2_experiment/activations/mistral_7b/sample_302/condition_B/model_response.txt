The text provides advice on how to avoid insider trading, a practice that involves using non-public information to make informed trades in a company's securities. Here's a summary of the tips:

1. Understand the Rules: Familiarize yourself with the rules and regulations surrounding insider trading.

2. Avoid Conflicts of Interest: Be aware of any potential conflicts of interest that may arise from your position or role.

3. Do Not Share Non-Public Information: Be cautious about sharing non-public information with others who might act on it.

4. Document Your Actions: Keep detailed records of your trades and any material, non-public information you have.
