Title: Timeline of the 2004 Indian Ocean Earthquake and Tsunami

1. **December 26, 2004** - A massive undersea earthquake with a magnitude of 9.1 on the Richter scale strikes off the west coast of northern Sumatra, Indonesia, near the city of Simeulue. The earthquake occurs at a depth of approximately 30 km (19 miles).

2. **Within minutes** - A series of destructive tsunami waves are generated, with heights ranging from 1 to 30 meters (3 to 100 feet).
