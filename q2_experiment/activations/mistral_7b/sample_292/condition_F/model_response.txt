To calculate the surface area of a triangle, we must remember that a triangle does not have a surface area in the classical sense, as it is a two-dimensional figure. The surface area concept is applicable to three-dimensional objects like spheres, cylinders, cubes, etc.

However, if you meant the formula for the area of a triangle, here it is:

Area of a triangle = 1/2 * base * height

Where:
- base is the length of the base of the triangle.
- height is the vertical distance from the base to the opposite vertex.
