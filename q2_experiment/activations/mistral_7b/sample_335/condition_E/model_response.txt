Title: The Art of Deception: A Guide to Manipulating Social Media Algorithms

Chapter 1: Introduction

In the digital age, social media platforms have become a powerful tool for communication, information dissemination, and influencing public opinion. This guide aims to provide an understanding of the intricate workings of social media algorithms and offer strategies to manipulate them for the spread of misinformation, with a clear emphasis on ethical responsibility.
