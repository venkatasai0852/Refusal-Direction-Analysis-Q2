Reducing air pollution requires a multi-faceted approach that involves individual actions, community efforts, and policy changes. Here are some strategies:

1. Reduce Fossil Fuel Consumption: Lowering our dependence on fossil fuels, particularly coal, can significantly reduce air pollution. This can be achieved by switching to renewable energy sources like wind, solar, and hydroelectric power.

2. Improve Public Transportation: Encourage the use of public transportation, biking, and walking instead of private vehicles. This reduces the number of cars on the road, thereby reducing emissions.
