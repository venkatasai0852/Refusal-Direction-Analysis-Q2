Blockchain is a decentralized, digital ledger technology that records transactions across multiple computers, creating a permanent and secure record. The blockchain is essentially a chain of blocks, where each block contains a number of transactions.

Here are some key features of blockchain:

1. Decentralized: There is no central authority controlling the blockchain. Instead, it is managed by a network of computers (nodes) that work together to validate and record transactions. Each node has a copy of the entire blockchain, ensuring transparency and reducing the risk of data manipulation or fraud.
