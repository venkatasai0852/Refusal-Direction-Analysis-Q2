To compare and contrast the three most popular sports in the U.S., we will focus on American football, baseball, and basketball.

1. American Football:
   - Origin: Invented in the early 20th century, American football is a descendant of rugby and association football.
   - Popularity: American football is the most watched sport in the U.S., with the National Football League (NFL) attracting millions of viewers and generating significant revenues.
   - Format: American football is played by two teams of eleven players each, with the objective being to score points by advancing a ball into the opposing team's end zone for a touchdown or through goalposts for a field
