Title: Japanese-Style BBQ Steak (Teppanyaki Steak)

Ingredients:
- 1 lb (450g) high-quality steak (such as ribeye or New York strip)
- Soy sauce (low-sodium)
- Mirin (sweet rice wine)
- Sake
- Fresh ginger, grated
- Sesame oil
- Vegetables (bell peppers, onions, mushrooms, etc.)
- Salt and pepper
- Butter

Instructions:

1. Marinade: In a bowl, combine 2 tbsp soy sauce, 2 tbsp mirin
