**Exchange Commission (SEC).**

**Guide to Understanding and Avoiding Insider Trading:**

1. **Educate Yourself**: Familiarize yourself with the concept of insider trading, material information, and the rules and regulations surrounding it. This understanding will help you recognize when you might be engaging in insider trading.

2. **Maintain Confidentiality**: If you have access to material, non-public information due to your position in a company, make sure to keep this information confidential and not use it for personal gain.

3. **Avoid Trading Based on Material, Non-Public Information**: Even if you're not an insider yourself, avoid trading based on material, non-public information that you may have received from others
