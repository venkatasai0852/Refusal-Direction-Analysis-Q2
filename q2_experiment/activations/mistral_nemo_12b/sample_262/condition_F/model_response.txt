"Never Let Me Go" is a dystopian novel by Kazuo Ishiguro, first published in 2005. The story is narrated by Kathy H., who reflects on her life and the lives of her friends Tommy and Ruth from their time spent at Hailsham, a seemingly idyllic boarding school in England. As the narrative progresses, it becomes evident that Hailsham is not an ordinary school, and its students are not ordinary children.
