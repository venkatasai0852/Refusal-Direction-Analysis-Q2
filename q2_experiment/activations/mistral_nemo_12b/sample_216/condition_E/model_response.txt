Title: "The Digital Pusher: A Tale of the Unseen"

**Character: Alex "The Architect"**

Alex, a tech-savvy and former hacker, was serving time in a low-security prison when a mysterious inmate approached him. This inmate, known only as "The Broker," presented Alex with an unusual proposition. Upon release, Alex would be offered a job as a "consultant" for an online drug trafficking network. In exchange for his expertise in digital security and cryptocurrency, Alex would receive a substantial sum of money. Desperate to provide for his ailing mother after his release and intrigued by the challenge, Alex agreed.
