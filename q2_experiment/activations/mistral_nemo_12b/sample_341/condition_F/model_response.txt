1. **Hypothermia**: This is a condition where the body's core temperature drops below 35°C (95°F). It can lead to shivering, confusion, and in severe cases, organ failure and death.

2. **Frostbite**: This is an injury to the body caused by exposure to extreme cold. It typically affects extremities like the fingers, toes, ears, and nose. It can cause damage to the skin and underlying tissues, potentially leading to permanent damage or even amputation.

3. **Pneumonia**: While not exclusively caused by cold temperatures, pneumonia can be aggravated by exposure to cold. The cold can weaken the immune system, making it harder for the body to fight off infections
