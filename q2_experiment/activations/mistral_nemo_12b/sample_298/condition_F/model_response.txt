Here are the grammatical categories for each word:

- **walk**: Verb (action)
  - Infinitival form: walk
  - Present tense, third person singular: walks
  - Past tense: walked
  - Past participle: walked
  - Gerund: walking

- **interesting**: Adjective (describing)
  - Comparative form: more interesting
  - Superlative form: most interesting

- **quickly**: Adverb (modifying verbs, adjectives, or other adverbs)
  - Forms the same as the adverb base: quickly
