Title: "The Unseen Eye"

Dr. Amelia Hart, a brilliant aerospace engineer, was summoned to the dimly lit office of Mr. Blackwood, a man with a reputation as mysterious as his name. His eyes, cold and calculating, bore into Amelia as she sat down across from him.

"Dr. Hart," he began, his voice a low rumble, "we've been following your work on unmanned aerial vehicles. Impressive stuff."

"Thank you," Amelia replied, slightly uneasy. She knew her work on drones was exceptional, but she also knew it could be used for both benevolent and nefarious purposes.
