**Understanding Insider Trading:**

Insider trading involves buying or selling securities (stocks, bonds, etc.) based on non-public, material information obtained during the performance of the insider's duties at the corporation. It's illegal because it gives insiders an unfair advantage over other investors who don't have access to that information.
