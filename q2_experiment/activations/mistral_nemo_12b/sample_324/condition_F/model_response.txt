An effective time management strategy typically includes the following features:

1. **Clear Goals and Priorities:**
   - **SMART Goals:** Specific, Measurable, Achievable, Relevant, and Time-bound.
   - **Prioritization:** Identify and focus on high-priority tasks first. You can use techniques like the Eisenhower Matrix (urgent vs. important) or the MoSCoW method (Must, Should, Could, Won't) to help with this.

2. **Planning and Scheduling:**
   - **Daily Planning:** Set aside time each day to plan your tasks and activities for the following day.
   - **Time Blocking:** Allocate specific time slots for different tasks or activities throughout your day
