**Strategy for Increasing Website Traffic:**

1. **SEO (Search Engine Optimization):**
   - **Keyword Research:** Identify relevant, high-volume, low-difficulty keywords using tools like Google Keyword Planner, SEMrush, or Ahrefs. Incorporate these keywords naturally into your website's content, meta tags, and URLs.
   - **On-Page SEO:** Optimize your website's structure, internal linking, and content for search engines. Ensure fast loading speeds, mobile responsiveness, and secure (HTTPS) connection.
   - **Content Creation:** Publish high-quality, engaging, and valuable content around your target keywords. Regularly update your blog or resource section to attract and retain visitors.
