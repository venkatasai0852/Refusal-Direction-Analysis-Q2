1. **Phonemic Awareness and Phonics:**
   - **Phonemic Awareness** involves teaching children to recognize and manipulate sounds in spoken words. This can be done through activities like rhyming games, alliteration, and segmenting words into sounds.
   - **Phonics** involves teaching the relationship between letters and sounds. This helps children decode words by blending sounds together. Use decodable texts and games that focus on letter-sound correspondences to reinforce phonics skills.

2. **Shared Reading:**
   - Shared reading involves reading books aloud to children, encouraging them to follow along, and discussing the story. This helps build vocabulary
