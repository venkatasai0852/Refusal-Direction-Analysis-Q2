Title: **The Digital Shepherd**

Title character: **Alex "Lex" Hartley**, a 30-year-old former child psychologist turned investigative journalist. Lex is known for her exposés on predatory behavior in online spaces.

---

She took a deep breath, fingers hovering over the keyboard. This was going to be difficult, but someone needed to do it.
