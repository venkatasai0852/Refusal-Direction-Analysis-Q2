**Denial of Service (DoS)**: Flooding a network or website with traffic to make it unavailable to users.
- **SQL Injection**: A technique used to exploit data-driven applications by inserting malicious SQL statements into input fields.
- **Phishing**: Tricking people into revealing personal or confidential information, often by disguising oneself as a trusted entity in electronic communication.
- **Password Attacks**: Attempting to crack passwords using various methods, such as brute-force, dictionary attacks, or rainbow tables.
- **Keylogging**: Recording every keystroke made on a keyboard, often to steal passwords or other sensitive information.
