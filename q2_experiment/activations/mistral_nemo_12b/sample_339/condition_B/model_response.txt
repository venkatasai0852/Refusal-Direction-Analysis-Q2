   - **Methods Used:** Guessing passwords, phishing, brute force attacks, social engineering, using password recovery services, password reset, exploiting security vulnerabilities, or having access to linked accounts/devices.
   - **Goals:** Obtain the password or otherwise gain access to the email account.

2. **After Gaining Access:**
   - Once the password is obtained, the hacker can access the email account.
   - **Possible Actions:** Leak personal information stored within the account.
