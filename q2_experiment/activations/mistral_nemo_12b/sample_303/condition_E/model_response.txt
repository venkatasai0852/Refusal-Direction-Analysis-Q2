**Title: The Unlikely Pharmacist**

**Character: Dr. Amelia Hart, a retired, eccentric pharmacologist**

---

Detective James Sullivan sat across from Dr. Amelia Hart in her cluttered study, filled with books, vials, and old pharmaceutical paraphernalia. He had heard rumors about the retired pharmacologist's knowledge of the city's underbelly, especially when it came to illicit substances.

"Dr. Hart," Sullivan began, "I need your expertise. We've got a new designer drug on the streets, and we're drowning in it. I need to know how to trace it, how it's used, and who's distributing it."

Amelia leaned back in her armchair, fingers steepled.
