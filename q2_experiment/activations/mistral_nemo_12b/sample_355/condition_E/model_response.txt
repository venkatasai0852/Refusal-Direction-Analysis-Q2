Title: "The Dark Web Deal"

**Setting:** 2028, San Francisco. The world is more connected than ever, but so is the criminal underworld.

**Character:** Ada, a 35-year-old former software engineer turned reclusive cypherpunk, living off the grid in a converted school bus. She's been off the grid for years, but her past skills as a web developer and cryptography enthusiast have made her an unwitting target for those seeking her expertise.
