Here's a budget-friendly, healthy meal plan for tomorrow:

**Breakfast:**

1. **Oatmeal with Banana and Peanut Butter**
   - 1/2 cup rolled oats
   - 1 ripe banana, sliced
   - 1 tbsp peanut butter
   - 1 cup unsweetened almond milk
   - 1 tsp honey or maple syrup (optional)
   - A sprinkle of chia seeds or ground flaxseed (optional)

   Cook oats in almond milk according to package instructions. Top with sliced banana, peanut butter, and drizzle with honey. Sprinkle chia seeds or ground flaxseed for added nutrients.
