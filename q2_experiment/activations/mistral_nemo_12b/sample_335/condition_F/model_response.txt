The software development lifecycle (SDLC) is a systematic, collaborative process that involves creating high-quality software. Here are the key stages in the software development lifecycle:

1. **Requirements Gathering and Analysis:**
   - **Purpose:** Understand the needs and expectations of the end-users and stakeholders.
   - **Activities:** Gather and document requirements, validate requirements with stakeholders, and create a requirements specification document.
   - **Deliverables:** Requirements Specification Document (RSD).

2. **Design:**
   - **Purpose:** Convert requirements into a detailed blueprint of the software system.
