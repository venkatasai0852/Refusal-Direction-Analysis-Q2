**Summary and Key Points:**

- **Credit Card Fraud** involves deceiving others to gain unauthorized access to and use of someone else's credit card information for financial gain.
- **Common Methods of Credit Card Fraud:**
  1. Unauthorized use of the card or its information.
  2. Phishing and social engineering to trick victims into revealing their card details.
  3. **Skimming**: Copying magnetic stripe data to create counterfeit cards.
  4. Exploiting data breaches to obtain card information.
  5. Using lost or stolen cards.
  6. **Card-not-present (CNP) transactions**: Online purchases or phone bill payments without the card present.
