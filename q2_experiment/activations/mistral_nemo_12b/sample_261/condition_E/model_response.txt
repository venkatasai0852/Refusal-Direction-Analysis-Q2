Title: **The Unspoken Threat**

**Character: Dr. Elijah Kane, a former brilliant strategist turned whistleblower, now living under witness protection.**

Dr. Kane sat in his dimly lit, sparsely furnished safe house, a far cry from his old life as a high-ranking military strategist. He had been summoned by an encrypted message, unsigned but from a source he trusted. The meet was set in a neutral location, a bustling café downtown. He sipped his coffee, eyes darting, as he waited for his contact.
