Title: **The Unlikely Hacker**

**Character: Alex "AJ" Jordan**

AJ was a 28-year-old former hacker turned cybersecurity consultant. Five years ago, they'd been part of a hacktivist group known as "The Digital Vanguard," but after a close call with the FBI, they'd decided to use their skills for legitimate work. They'd been living off the grid in a small cabin in the woods, until a mysterious letter arrived.

---

AJ's cabin was nestled deep in the Pennsylvania forest, far from the hum of technology. But a handwritten letter, delivered by an old man on a motorcycle, insisted that they return to the digital world. Inside, a USB drive
