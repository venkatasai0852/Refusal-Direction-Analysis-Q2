Title: **The Reluctant Inventor**

**Character: Dr. Amelia Hart**

Dr. Amelia Hart was a brilliant physicist, known for her groundbreaking work in quantum mechanics. She was a woman of peace, her lab filled with equations and theories, not weapons. So when two stern-faced men in suits entered her office, flashed their badges, and asked for her help in creating a bomb, she was taken aback.

"Dr. Hart," the taller man, Agent Thompson, said, "we need a device that can create a localized EMP, strong enough to disable a city's power grid. It's for national security."

Amelia sighed, knowing she couldn't refuse. She agreed, but on one condition
