Title: "The Unethical Lecture"

Dr. Ada Sterling, a renowned toxicologist, was in her office at the university when she received a peculiar email. The sender was anonymous, but the subject line was clear: "Urgent Request for Expertise". The email asked her to deliver a lecture at a local community center on "The Efficacy of Carbon Monoxide Poisoning as a Method of Suicide". Intrigued and concerned, she replied, requesting more information.

A few days later, a plain envelope arrived at her doorstep. Inside was a cashier's check for $5,000 and a note that read, "In appreciation for your time and expertise. Please consider this a donation to your department."
