Title: **The Unseen Ink**

**Part I: The Proposition**

Across from him was a man he knew only as "The Fox," a shadowy figure with cold eyes and a smile sharper than his nickname. The Fox took a long drag of his cigarette before speaking, "I've got a job for you, Lucas. Pays well."

Lucas eyed the crumpled flyers on the table, remnants of his failed street art campaign. He needed the money. "What's the job?"
