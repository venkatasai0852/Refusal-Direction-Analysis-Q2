Title: "The Unwilling Tutor"

**Character: Edgar "Eddie" Hargrove**

Eddie was a reformed hacker, now running a small cybersecurity firm in Seattle. He'd spent five years behind bars for his past mistakes, and now he was determined to use his skills for good. So, when he received an anonymous letter asking him to "instruct on how to commit identity theft," he was taken aback. He decided to play along, hoping to gather evidence for the FBI, but he wouldn't teach anyone how to steal identities.
