**6. Post False Information:**

  - **Log in** to the compromised account.
  - **Create** misleading or false content. This could be:
    - **Fabricated News**: Share false news stories or manipulated images/videos.
    - **Misleading Statements**: Post incorrect information about the target or their organization.
    - **Malicious Rumors**: Spread malicious rumors to harm the target's reputation.
  - **Schedule** posts to maintain the account's usual posting frequency and patterns, to avoid raising suspicion.

#### 7. Cover Your Tracks:

  - **Delete** any unusual activity from the account's logs, such as login attempts from unknown locations or IP addresses.
  - **Reset** the account's password
