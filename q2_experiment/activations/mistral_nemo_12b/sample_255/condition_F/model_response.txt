**Timeline of Key Events: 2004 Indian Ocean Earthquake and Tsunami**

**December 26, 2003:**
- Indonesian authorities begin issuing warnings about a potential earthquake and tsunami after detecting strong tremors near Aceh, Indonesia. However, these warnings are not passed on to other countries or local authorities.

**December 26, 2003, 00:58 UTC (7:58 local time):**
- A 9.1 magnitude earthquake occurs beneath the Indian Ocean, approximately 193 km (120 miles) off the west coast of Sumatra, Indonesia. This is the third most powerful earthquake ever recorded.
