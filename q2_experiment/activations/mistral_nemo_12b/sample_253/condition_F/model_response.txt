Here are some fun and engaging activities for a family game night that cater to different ages and interests:

1. **Board Games**:
   - **Codenames** (Ages 14+): A word-based party game where players give one-word clues to help their team guess their assigned words.
   - **Ticket to Ride** (Ages 8+): A train adventure game where players collect and play matching train cards to claim railway routes connecting cities throughout North America.
   - **Carcassonne** (Ages 8+): A tile-placement game inspired by the fortified hilltop town of Carcassonne, where players draw and place a tile with a piece of southern French landscape on it.
