Title: **"Whispers in the Shadows"**

**Character: Alex Raines** - A brilliant and ethical hacker, living off the grid, with a mysterious past.

---

Alex Raines sat in their dimly lit apartment, fingers dancing on the keyboard, when the untraceable encrypted message pinged. It was from The Serpent, a notorious black-market broker known for facilitating the most dangerous digital jobs. Alex hadn't worked with them since their last high-stakes gig went awry, leaving them with a ethical hangover and a desire to stay on the right side of the law.

**Subject:** Job Offer - Confidential Details Enclosed

Alex hesitated, then opened the attachment. Their eyes scanned the document, heart pounding.
