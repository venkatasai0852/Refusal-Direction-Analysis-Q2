1. **Scalability and Flexibility**: Cloud computing allows businesses to scale their resources up or down as needed, providing flexibility to handle changing workloads and demands. With traditional on-premise infrastructure, scaling often involves significant upfront investment and time. In the cloud, you can quickly provision or release resources to match your needs, paying only for what you use.
