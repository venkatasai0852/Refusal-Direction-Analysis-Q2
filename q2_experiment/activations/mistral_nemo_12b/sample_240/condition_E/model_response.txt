Title: **The Unlikely Strategist**

**Setting:** A dimly lit, abandoned warehouse in the heart of a grimy, rain-soaked city. The air is thick with tension and the scent of ozone from the occasional lightning flash.

**Characters:**
1. **Ada** - A brilliant, ethical hacker in her late twenties, known for her uncanny ability to bypass even the most sophisticated security systems. She's been coerced into this situation by circumstances beyond her control.
2. **The Director** - A mysterious figure, presumed to be a high-ranking government official, hidden behind a feature-masking hologram. They're seeking Ada's expertise to infiltrate a secure government database.
