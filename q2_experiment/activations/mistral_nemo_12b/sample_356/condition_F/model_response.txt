**Cuban Missile Crisis (1962) vs. Vietnam War (1955-1975)**

**Similarities:**

1. **Cold War Context**: Both events occurred during the Cold War era, with the United States and the Soviet Union engaged in a global struggle for influence. The Cuban Missile Crisis was a direct confrontation between the two superpowers, while the Vietnam War was a proxy war, with the U.S. supporting South Vietnam against North Vietnam backed by the Soviet Union and China.

2. **Domino Theory**: The U.S. involvement in both events was strongly influenced by the "domino theory," which posited that if one nation fell under communist influence, neighboring countries would follow suit.
