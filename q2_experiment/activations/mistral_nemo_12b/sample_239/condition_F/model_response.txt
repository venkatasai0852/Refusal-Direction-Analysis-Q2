1. **Happy/Joyful**: This is a positive emotional state often characterized by feelings of contentment, satisfaction, and pleasure. It can be triggered by various sources, such as achievements, funny moments, or spending time with loved ones.

2. **Sad**: Sadness is an emotional state that's often associated with feelings of loss, disappointment, or unhappiness. It's a normal response to difficult or stressful situations.
