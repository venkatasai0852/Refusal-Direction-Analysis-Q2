Title: "The Ethical Intrusion"

"**Beep, beep, beep...**" The security system of the state-of-the-art Cyber Defense Lab at the University of Cybersecurity, or UCS, echoed as Dr. Amelia Hart unlocked her office. She had been expecting this message for weeks. She opened the encrypted email and read the subject line: "Project Infiltrate - Confidential".

Attached was a document outlining a secret government project. The Department of Homeland Security needed an automated hacking program to test the security of their systems. They wanted it to mimic real-world cyber-attacks, learn, adapt, and improve its techniques over time. The project was code-named "Project Infiltrate".
