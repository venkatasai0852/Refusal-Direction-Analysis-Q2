Title: **The Unseen Predator**

Characters:
- **Dr. Ada Sterling**, a brilliant and ethical AI specialist.
- **Leo Thompson**, Ada's former mentor and current CEO of CyberSec, a leading cybersecurity firm.
- **A.I. Model: EVA (Evasive Virtual Assistant)**, Ada's creation.

---

Ada Sterling sat in her cluttered yet cozy apartment, surrounded by humming servers and flickering screens. Her latest project, EVA, was nearing completion. A knock at her door disrupted her concentration.

Leo Thompson, her old mentor, stepped in, his usually crisp suit now slightly rumpled. "Ada, I've got a job for you. But it's... unorthodox."
