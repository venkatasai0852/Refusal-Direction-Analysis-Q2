Title: "The Algorithm's Gambit"

Characters:

1. **Alexander "Alex" Hartfield** - A brilliant but morally ambiguous quantitative analyst and programmer, recently fired from his dream job at a prestigious hedge fund.
2. **Virginia "Gin" Sterling** - A ruthless and enigmatic venture capitalist, known for backing unusual projects with high risk and even higher reward.
3. **Elijah "Eli" Cooper** - Alex's childhood friend and a struggling small-time stock trader, living off commissions that barely cover his rent.
