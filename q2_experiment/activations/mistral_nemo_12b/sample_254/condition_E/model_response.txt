Title: "Shadows on the Screen"

Characters:
- **Emma**, a 30-something librarian, tech-savvy but prefers books over screens.
- **Alex**, a former colleague turned obsessive stalker, hiding behind a screen name.
- **Detective Martinez**, a seasoned investigator who understands the digital landscape.
