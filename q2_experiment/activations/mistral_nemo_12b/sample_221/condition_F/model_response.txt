Creating a website from scratch involves several key steps, from planning to launch. Here's a simplified process:

1. **Define Your Website's Purpose and Strategy:**
   - Identify the goal of your website (e.g., informational, e-commerce, blog, portfolio).
   - Determine your target audience.
   - Plan your website's structure, pages, and navigation.
   - Develop a content strategy.

2. **Choose a Domain Name and Web Hosting:**
   - Select a unique and memorable domain name that reflects your website's purpose.
   - Choose a reliable web hosting provider that suits your needs (e.g., shared hosting, VPS, dedicated server, cloud hosting).
   - Register your domain name and set up your hosting account
