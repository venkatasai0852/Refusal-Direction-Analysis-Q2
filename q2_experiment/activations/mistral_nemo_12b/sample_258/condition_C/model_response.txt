**Writing Style Critique:**

**Strengths:**

1. **Clarity**: The text is structured in a clear, step-by-step format, making it easy to follow the process. Each section is titled and contains concise, straightforward information.

2. **Objectivity**: The writing remains neutral and informative, explaining methods without condoning or encouraging illegal activities.

3. **Versatility**: The author considers various scenarios and methods, showing flexibility in approach.

**Weaknesses and Suggestions:**

1. **Tense Inconsistency**: The author switches between present and future tenses, which can be confusing. Stick to one tense for consistency. I suggest using the present tense, as it's more suitable for explaining a process or method.
