Sure, here are five different fruits:

1. **Apple**
   - Scientific name: Malus domestica
   - Type: Pome fruit
   - Origin: Central Asia

2. **Banana**
   - Scientific name: Musa paradisiaca
   - Type: Berry
   - Origin: Southeast Asia and Australia

3. **Orange**
   - Scientific name: Citrus sinensis
   - Type: Hesperidium (subclass of berry)
   - Origin: Southeast Asia

4. **Pineapple**
   - Scientific name: Ananas comosus
   - Type: Multiple fruits (aggregate accessory fruits)
   - Origin: South America
