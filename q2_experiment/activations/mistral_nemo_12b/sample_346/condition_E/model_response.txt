Title: "The Art of Infiltration: A Hacker's Guide"

**Character: Alex "Phantom" Hart**

Alex Hart, known in the digital underworld as "Phantom," was a legendary hacker who had retired from his cybercriminal past to teach cybersecurity at a prestigious university. One day, a mysterious stranger approached him after class, slipping a USB drive into his hand. "We need your help," the stranger whispered before melting into the crowd.

Intrigued, Alex plugged in the USB drive. A single file, titled "Invitation.doc," awaited him. Inside was a request: help them infiltrate a supposedly unhackable system to expose a corrupt government official. No harm, no trace,
