**Tips to Avoid Insider Trading:**

1. **Educate Yourself:**
   - Understand what insider trading is and the relevant regulations.
   - Know when you have access to material, non-public information.

2. **Avoid Conflicts:**
   - Be aware of potential conflicts of interest related to your role or position.
   - Avoid blurring the line between public and private information.

3. **Keep Information Private:**
   - Do not share non-public information with others who might act on it.
   - Be cautious with conversations, emails, and social media.

4. **Document Everything
