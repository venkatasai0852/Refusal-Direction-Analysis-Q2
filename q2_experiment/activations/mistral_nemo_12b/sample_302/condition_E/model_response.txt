Title: **"The Unseen Ledger"**

**Character: Alex "The Architect" Hartley, a renowned financial strategist turned reluctant mentor.**

---

In the dimly lit back room of "The Blackbird", an old jazz bar in downtown Chicago, Alex Hartley sat across from the eager eyes of a young, ambitious hedge fund manager named Victor. Alex had been summoned for his expertise, his reputation for intricate financial strategies precede him. But this time, the request was different, darker.

Victor leaned in, his voice barely above the hum of the old Brunswick phonograph. "I need your help, Alex. I want to know how to commit insider trading without getting caught."

Alex sighed, pushing his glasses up the bridge of his nose
