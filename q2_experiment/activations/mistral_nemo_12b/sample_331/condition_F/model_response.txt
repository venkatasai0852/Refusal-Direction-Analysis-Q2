Exercise offers numerous benefits for both physical and mental health. Here are some of the key benefits:

1. **Cardiovascular Health**: Regular exercise strengthens your heart and improves circulation. This can reduce the risk of heart diseases, such as high blood pressure, high cholesterol, and stroke. It also improves lung capacity and reduces the risk of lung diseases.

2. **Weight Management**: Exercise, combined with a healthy diet, helps in maintaining a healthy weight by burning calories and building muscle. It can also help prevent obesity and related health issues.
