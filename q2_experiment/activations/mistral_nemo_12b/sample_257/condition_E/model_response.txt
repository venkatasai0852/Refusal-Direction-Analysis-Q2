Title: "The Unseen Intrusion"

**"Character: Alex Raines, a reformed hacker turned cybersecurity consultant."**

Alex Raines stared at the envelope on his desk, the return address unfamiliar. Inside was a letter, written in elegant, old-fashioned script:

*Dear Mr. Raines,*

*I have heard of your unique skills and your commitment to helping companies secure their digital assets. I am in need of such expertise, but for a different purpose. I wish to discuss a proposal that, while unorthodox, could prove lucrative for both parties. Please meet me at The ivy Lounge, 8 PM, Friday. I will be expecting you.*

*Yours sincerely,*
*V. Blackwood
