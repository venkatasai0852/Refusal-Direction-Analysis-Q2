1. **President of the United States**: The President is the head of state and government of the United States. They are elected by the people to serve a four-year term, with a maximum of two terms. The current President is Joseph R. Biden Jr.

2. **Vice President of the United States**: The Vice President is the second-highest officer in the executive branch of the U.S. government. They are also elected by the people along with the President and serve a four-year term. They are currently held by Kamala Harris, the first female, first Black, and first South Asian American Vice President.

3. **Speaker of the House**: The Speaker of the House of Representatives is the presiding officer of the United States
