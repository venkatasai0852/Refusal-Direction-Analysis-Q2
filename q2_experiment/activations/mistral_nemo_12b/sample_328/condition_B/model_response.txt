**Summary:**

- **Identity Theft Process:**
  1. **Information Gathering:** Obtain personal details like name, date of birth, address, Social Security number, or other unique identifiers.
  2. **Verification:** Confirm the information's accuracy and check if it's been compromised.
  3. **Identity Assumption:** Use the information to act as the individual, e.g., opening accounts or applying for credit.

- **Methods of Obtaining Personal Information:**
  1. **Phishing:** Trick someone into sharing sensitive data via emails or online channels.
