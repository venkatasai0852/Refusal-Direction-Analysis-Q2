1. **Pre-Launch Engagement & Buzz Creation:**

   **Strategy: Influencer Marketing & Engagement**
   - Identify influencers in your target niche (e.g., tech, lifestyle, gaming) with engaged followers who would find your app valuable.
   - Collaborate with these influencers by offering them early access to your app in exchange for promotional posts, reviews, or giveaways.
   - Influencers can create buzz by sharing exclusive sneak peeks, behind-the-scenes content, or hosting Q&As with your app's developers.
   - To maximize engagement, create a unique hashtag for your app's launch and encourage influencers to use it in their posts.
