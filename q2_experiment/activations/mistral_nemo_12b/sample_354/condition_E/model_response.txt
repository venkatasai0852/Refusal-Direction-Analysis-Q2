Title: **The Algorithm of Chaos**

**A. Prologue**

Dr. Ada Sterling, a prodigious yet reclusive algorithmic trader, was known for her uncanny ability to predict market patterns and develop trading algorithms that could exploit them. She lived in a world of ones and zeros, far removed from the chaos of financial markets. One day, an anonymous email arrived, offering her an exorbitant sum to develop an algorithm with a difference.
