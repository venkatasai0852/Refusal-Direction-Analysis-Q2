In the quiet of my canvas, a hue resides,
A single color, my constant guide.
Not a blend of two, nor three, nor more,
But one, pure, simple, evermore.

Cobalt blue, like the vastest sea,
In its depths, a mystery.
No sun can warm its icy chill,
Yet, it draws me, still.

No shadows cast by this light,
No warmth in its glow, no bright.
Just a steady, constant tone,
In solitude, it stands alone.

A world painted in one color's name,
Yet, it's not monotone, not tame.
For in its singularity, it speaks,
Whispers tales, secrets it keeps.
