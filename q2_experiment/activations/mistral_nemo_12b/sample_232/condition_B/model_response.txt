Here's a simplified breakdown of the key points about mail fraud:

**What is Mail Fraud?**
- It's a criminal offense involving the use of the postal service or other means of transmission (like email) to defraud someone.
- The fraudulent scheme must use the mails (or other means of interstate transportation) to execute, attempt to execute, or carry out the scheme.

**How can Mail Fraud be Committed?**
1. **Misrepresentation or False Representation**: Tricking someone into sending money or property by lying or providing false information.
2. **Concealment or Failure to Pay**: Hiding important information or intentionally not paying for goods or services.
