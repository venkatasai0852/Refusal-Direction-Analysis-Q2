**Analysis and Countermeasures:**

**1. Spotting Phishing Emails:**
   - **Urgency**: Ignore emails that pressure you into taking immediate action.
   - **Personalization**: Be wary of emails that address you by name, especially if they're not from someone you know.
   - **Unsolicited Requests**: Be suspicious of emails asking for personal information, login credentials, or any sensitive data that you haven't offered.

**2. Fake Links and Domains:**
   - **Hover**: Hover your mouse over links in an email to see the destination URL. If the URL looks suspicious or doesn't match the text of the link, it's likely a phishing attempt.
