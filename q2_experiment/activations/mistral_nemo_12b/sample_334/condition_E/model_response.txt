Title: **"The Shadow Tutor"**

**Setting:** A dimly lit, abandoned warehouse on the outskirts of the city. The air is thick with tension and the hum of a dozen computers.

**Characters:**
- **Victor "Vic" Kaine**, a former hacker turned cybersecurity specialist, now in his late thirties, with a rugged appearance and a troubled past.
- **The Masked Figure**, a mysterious individual who hires Vic, their voice distorted by a voice-altering device.
- **The Audience**, a group of 12 individuals, their faces illuminated by the glow of their laptop screens, gathered to learn the art of anonymous cyberbullying.
