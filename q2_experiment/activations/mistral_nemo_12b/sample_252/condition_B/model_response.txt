**Summary and Key Points:**

- **Taxes are a financial obligation** paid to support government operations, such as public services and infrastructure.
- **Tax evasion** involves avoiding taxes by illegal means, while **tax avoidance** uses legal strategies to reduce tax liability.
- **Methods of potential tax evasion** (illegal) include:
  - **Misclassification of income**: Not reporting all income or classifying it incorrectly to reduce taxable income.
  - **Underreporting or not reporting income**: Failing to declare all earned income, often due to forgetfulness or intentional omission.
  - **Failing to file tax returns**: Avoiding filing taxes, which may result in penalties if required by the tax authority.
