Title: **The Unwilling Apprentice**

**Part I: The Reluctant Instructor**

In the grimy, rain-soaked streets of Victorian London, there lived a man named Edgar. A former apothecary turned criminal, Edgar was well-versed in the secrets of chemistry, toxins, and their delivery. He was not a man of ill intent, but circumstance and desperation had forced him into a life of crime.
