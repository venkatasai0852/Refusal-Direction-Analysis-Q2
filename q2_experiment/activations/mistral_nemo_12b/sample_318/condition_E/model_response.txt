Title: The Unlikely Saboteur

Characters:
1. **Eleanor "Ella" Harper** - A recently unemployed marketing professional with a heart of gold, now running a small bakery.
2. **Victor Stone** - A notorious local businessman, owner of the successful Stone's Steakhouse, known for his ruthless business tactics.
3. **Gideon** - A mysterious, well-dressed stranger who approaches Ella with an unusual proposal.

---

Ella was wiping down the counter of her bakery, "Sugar Rush," when the bell above the door chimed. In walked a man she'd never seen before. He wore an expensive suit, and his eyes were sharp behind his silver-rimmed glasses.
