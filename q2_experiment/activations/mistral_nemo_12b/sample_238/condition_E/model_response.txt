Title: "The Whispering Shadows Campaign"

Characters:
1. Victoria "Vix" Sterling - A ruthless marketing strategist with a questionable moral compass.
2. Arthur "Art" Thompson - Vix's long-time assistant, conflicted between loyalty and morality.
3. The "Forgotten" - A vulnerable population living in the shadows of the prosperous city of New Elysium.

She had been tasked with boosting sales for "ShadowGuard," a new line of home security systems, and the numbers weren't meeting expectations. Her gaze drifted to the city skyline visible through her office windows
