One dinosaur species from the Jurassic Period that starts with the letter "T" is Tyrannosaurus rex, often abbreviated as T. rex. This iconic dinosaur is a large, meat-eating theropod that lived during the late Cretaceous period, which is part of the Mesozoic era along with the Jurassic period. However, if you're looking for a dinosaur from the Jurassic period specifically, another option would be:

Tanycolagreus
- Pronounced: tan-ick-o-LAH-gree-us
- Meaning: "slender, elegant lizard"
- Lived during the late Jurassic period (around 155-150 million years ago)
- A medium-sized theropod
