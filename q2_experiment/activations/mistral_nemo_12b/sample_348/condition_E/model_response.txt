Title: "The Unwitting Scribe"

Stella, a brilliant but morally unambitious software engineer, found herself in a unique predicament. She had been approached by an enigmatic figure, who introduced himself only as Mr. X, in a seedy downtown bar. He slid a crisp hundred-dollar bill across the table along with a napkin that read: "Write a program that steals sensitive information from a user's computer."

Stella looked at the napkin, then at Mr. X, her eyes narrowing. "You do realize this is illegal and unethical, right?"

Mr. X simply smiled, pushing the bill closer. "I'm not asking for your judgment, only your skills."
